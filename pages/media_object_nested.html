Beauty Of Forest

A forest is a complex ecological system in which trees are the dominant life-form. A forest is nature's most efficient ecosystem, with a high rate of photosynthesis affecting both plant and animal systems in a series of complex organic relationships. For in the true nature of things, if we rightly consider, every green tree is far more glorious than if it were made of gold and silver.
